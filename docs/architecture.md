## Executive Summary

This document describes an AI-powered voice calling system designed to perform wellness checks on elderly family members. The system combines:

- **Twilio** for telephony infrastructure
- **OpenAI Realtime API** for speech-to-text
- **Anthropic Claude** for conversational AI
- **ElevenLabs** for natural text-to-speech
- **OpenClaw** as the orchestration layer

The result: An AI assistant that can call your grandmother, introduce itself as a community health nurse, ask about her kidney health, and report back if anything seems concerning.

## Summary

This architecture demonstrates a practical, cost-effective approach to AI-powered elderly care:

| Aspect | Implementation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Accessibility** | Regular phone calls (no app needed) |
| **Natural Interaction** | ElevenLabs provides human-like voice |
| **Intelligence** | Claude understands context and health concerns |
| **Integration** | Fits into existing OpenClaw assistant ecosystem |
| **Cost** | ~$0.55 per 5-minute call |
| **Latency** | 3-5 seconds (acceptable for use case) |

The system successfully completed wellness check calls to an 80-year-old patient, introducing itself as "Sarah" the community health nurse and inquiring about kidney health (GFR levels), pain, and general wellbeing.
